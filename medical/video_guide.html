📹 Mohannad Medical Clinic - Video Creation Guide

Option 1: Use OBS Studio (Recommended for Professional Quality)

  1. Download OBS Studio: https://obsproject.com
  2. Create a new scene with your screen/camera
  3. Start recording a tour of your clinic (2-3 minutes)
  4. Export as MP4 format
  5. Place the video in assets/videos/ folder as clinic_tour.mp4

Option 2: Use iPhone/Android Phone

  1. Record a video of your clinic using your phone camera
  2. Add simple text overlays (Optional)
  3. Transfer video to your computer
  4. Save as assets/videos/clinic_tour.mp4

Option 3: Use a Video Editor (like DaVinci Resolve - Free)

  1. Download DaVinci Resolve: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve
  2. Import clinic tour footage
  3. Add background music and transitions
  4. Add text: "Mohannad Medical Clinic - Welcome"
  5. Add voiceover (Optional) with narration about services
  6. Export as MP4

📝 What Your Video Should Include:

  • ✓ 3-5 minute clinic tour showing facilities
  • ✓ Reception area and waiting room
  • ✓ Consultation rooms
  • ✓ Modern equipment
  • ✓ Staff interviews (Optional but recommended)
  • ✓ Professional background music
  • ✓ Text overlay with service descriptions
  • ✓ Clear voiceover about clinic services

Narrator: "Welcome to Mohannad Medical Clinic, located in the prestigious Al-Moruj District of Riyadh. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services with cutting-edge technology and experienced medical professionals."

"Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern operating rooms, intensive care units, and advanced diagnostic equipment. Our team of specialist doctors is committed to delivering the highest quality patient care."

Place your video file at:

assets/videos/clinic_tour.mp4
