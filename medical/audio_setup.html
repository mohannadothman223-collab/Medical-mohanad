Option 3: Record Your Own Voiceover

Using Audacity (Free Software)

  1. Download Audacity: https://www.audacityteam.org
  2. Open Audacity and create new project
  3. Click Record Button (red circle)
  4. Read your script clearly:
    "Welcome to Mohannad Medical Clinic, located in the beautiful Al-Moruj District of Riyadh. We are dedicated to providing world-class healthcare with experienced physicians and cutting-edge technology. Our team includes specialists in general medicine, pediatrics, cardiology, and more. We offer 24/7 emergency services, modern surgical facilities, and comprehensive diagnostic services. Trust us with your health. Book your appointment with us today."
  5. Stop Recording when done
  6. Export as MP3: File → Export → MP3
  7. Save as: voiceover.mp3
  8. Place in: assets/audio/ folder

🎯 Audio Integration Checklist

  • ☐ Record/Generate voiceover (2-3 minutes)
  • ☐ Export as MP3 format
  • ☐ Name file clearly (e.g., clinic_welcome.mp3)
  • ☐ Place in assets/audio/ folder
  • ☐ File size optimized (<10MB preferred)
  • ☐ Audio quality tested (clear and professional)
  • ☐ Download background music from recommended sites
  • ☐ Add audio player to video (optional)
  • ☐ Test all audio playback on different devices
  • ☐ Ensure proper volume levels

✨ Advanced: Add Audio to Video

Using DaVinci Resolve (Free)

  1. Download: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve
  2. Import your clinic video
  3. Import background music
  4. Import voiceover audio
  5. Arrange audio tracks on timeline
  6. Adjust audio levels and mixing
  7. Add transitions and effects
  8. Export as clinic_tour.mp4

Professional Audio Settings:

  • Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
  • Bit Depth: 16-bit or 24-bit
  • Format: MP3 or AAC
  • Bitrate: 128-320 kbps
  • Volume Level: -3dB to -6dB (safe for all devices)
