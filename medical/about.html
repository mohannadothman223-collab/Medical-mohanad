About Mohannad Medical Clinic

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Al-Moruj District

Our Story

Mohannad Medical Clinic was established with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to the residents of Riyadh and surrounding areas. With a commitment to excellence, patient care, and continuous innovation, we have grown to become one of the most trusted medical institutions in Al-Moruj District.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, high-quality healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and innovation, ensuring every patient receives personalized medical care in a comfortable environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading medical clinic in Riyadh, known for exceptional patient care, cutting-edge medical technology, and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals.
